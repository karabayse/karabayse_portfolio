Travelogue | Solo Project

Upon traveling, I found myself wanting to log my experiences without the burden of writing longhand entries at the end of the day. Users have the ability to electronically log travel experiences with a date stamp and geolocation. Technologies used: JavaScript, AngularJS, MongoDB, Mongoose, CSS3, Bootstrap, Node.js, Express.js, Google Maps API, Filestack API
